CCIEA PUBLICATIONS: Reports

A number of independent reports are produced using the IEA process, including: Annual Ecosystem Status Reports produced for the Pacific Fisheries Management Council; Overview and synthesis reports that developed the technical background for IEA in the California Current; and regionally specific reports that develop indicators and conceptual models for local, state, and federal partners.

Reports to the Pacific Fishery Management Council (PFMC)

The Pacific Fishery Management Council (PFMC) requests an annual report from the CCIEA team on the status and trends of key indicators of climate, oceanography, ecology, fisheries and other human activities, and human wellbeing in the California Current Ecosystem. This request is part of the PFMC's Fishery Ecosystem Plan (FEP). The CCIEA team presents these reports to the PFMC and its advisory bodies each March.
